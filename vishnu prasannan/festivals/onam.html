Onam

ONAM: THE FESTIVAL OF FLOWERS

Onam is the state festival of Kerala, a 10-day harvest festival held in August or September that commemorates the homecoming of the legendary King Mahabali and celebrates the beginning of the harvest season. The festival involves decorating homes with Pookalam (flower mats), enjoying a grand vegetarian feast called Onasadya, and participating in festivities like the Vallam Kali (boat races) and Pulikali (tiger dances).
